Whats New at GEHA at Arrowhead?

Hall of Honor

Among all the beautiful renovations happening in GEHA Arrowhead Kingdom, the Hall of Honor happens to be one of the crowning jewel attractions. Located on the first level of the stadium, the Hall of Honor is carpeted with displays of the Kansas City Chiefs History, the Superbowl trophies, and the honorees of the Kansas City Hall of Fame!
